about me

Hi there, my name is Sergii, I’m 30 years old and I’m junior Frontend developer. I was born and raised in Uzhgorod a lovely town in the west of Ukraine. During school as any other boy I loved to play football, swim and do all other types of sports but my real passion was math. Therefore after school I continued my education in university doing Master in “Accounting and Auditing”. After graduation I did my compulsory military service in Presidential Honor Guard Battalion in Kyiv. In 2012 I started my service in State Tax Service of Ukraine, where after few years I became senior state auditor-inspector. But after some time I started to realize that I don’t see myself at government work for rest of my life, it’s not interesting for me not challenging enough and I simply don’t like it. That’s why I decided to try something what was my old dream, I spent a year to learn and train myself in frontend development, I did number of courses, video lessons, labs and small projects and now I feel that I ready for the real challenge in a real project.
